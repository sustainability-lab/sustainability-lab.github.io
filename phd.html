PhD Positions

We are always looking for strong PhD students to join us in building AI solutions for real-world sustainability challenges.

Write to Prof. Nipun Batra IIT Gandhinagar PhD Portal

Research Areas

Our PhD students work on problems at the intersection of AI and sustainability, using state-of-the-art methods including Large Language Models (LLMs), Vision-Language Models (VLMs), computer vision, and advanced ML techniques.

AI for Environmental Monitoring

Computer vision for satellite imagery analysis, brick kiln detection, air quality inference using Gaussian processes, LLMs for climate policy analysis.

Health Sensing & Digital Health

VLMs for medical imaging, respiratory monitoring (SpiroMask), energy expenditure estimation from thermal imagery (JoulesEye).

Energy Analytics

Non-intrusive load monitoring (NILM), time-series forecasting, smart grid optimization with deep learning.

Machine Learning for Social Good

Multi-modal models for sustainability, LLM-powered tools for air quality stakeholders, uncertainty quantification in deep learning.

What You Get

  • Funding: PhD fellowships including PMRF, institute fellowships, and project funding
  • Compute: NVIDIA A100 GPUs, RTX A5000s — 424GB+ GPU memory across lab servers
  • Mentorship: Regular 1-on-1 meetings, structured group meetings, and a collaborative lab culture
  • Collaboration: Opportunities to work with international researchers and industry partners (Google, Cisco, Microsoft, NVIDIA)
  • Publication venues: Top venues including NeurIPS, UbiComp/IMWUT, BuildSys, AAAI, and more
  • Community: A supportive lab with 50+ alumni now at leading institutions and companies worldwide

How to Apply

1

Write to Prof. Nipun Batra

2

Discuss research fit

We’ll have a conversation to explore alignment between your interests and our ongoing projects.

3

Apply to IIT Gandhinagar

Submit your formal application through the IIT Gandhinagar PhD admissions portal. Applications are accepted twice a year.
