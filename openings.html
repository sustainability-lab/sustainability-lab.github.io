Our Vision

At the Sustainability Lab, we envision a world where artificial intelligence serves as a powerful force for environmental and social good. Our mission is to develop innovative, data-driven solutions that address the most pressing sustainability challenges of our time.

We believe in the transformative potential of AI to monitor environmental changes, optimize resource usage, improve healthcare accessibility, and create sustainable systems that benefit both people and the planet. Through rigorous research, collaborative partnerships, and real-world applications, we strive to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and meaningful societal impact.

PhD Positions

PhD in Computer Science & Engineering

Pursue cutting-edge research in AI for sustainability using state-of-the-art technologies including Large Language Models (LLMs), Vision-Language Models (VLMs), computer vision, and advanced ML techniques applied to real-world sustainability challenges.

Research Areas & Technologies:

  • AI for Environmental Monitoring: Computer vision for satellite imagery analysis, LLMs for climate policy analysis
  • Health Sensing & Digital Health: VLMs for medical imaging, ML for respiratory monitoring
  • Energy Analytics: Time-series forecasting, smart grid optimization with deep learning
  • Machine Learning for Social Good: Multi-modal models for sustainability applications

Application Process: Write to Prof. Nipun Batra to discuss research fit. Applications to IIT Gandhinagar are accepted twice a year through the official portal.

Summer Research Internships

Summer Research Internship (SRIP 2026)

Join our Summer Internship Program and work on exciting projects using cutting-edge AI technologies in the following domains:
